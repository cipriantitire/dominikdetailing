## Design Context

### Users
Busy professionals and luxury car owners in London and surrounding areas. They value convenience (mobile service comes to them), quality (premium not basic), and trust (letting someone work on their expensive vehicle). The job-to-be-done is booking a high-end car detailing service without friction — they want to feel confident their car is in expert hands.

### Brand Personality
Premium. Meticulous. Trustworthy. The brand should feel like a boutique concierge service, not a high-volume car wash. Voice is confident and refined, never salesy or pushy.

3-word personality: **Premium. Precise. Personal.**

Emotional goals: Evoke luxury (this is a treat for your car) and trust (we handle everything with care).

### Aesthetic Direction
Premium dark detailing aesthetic. Deep charcoal backgrounds with deep blue primary CTAs and warm gold accents. Avoid generic "AI slop" — no cyan/purple gradients, no neon glows, no glassmorphism. Should feel like a high-end automotive studio's digital presence: clean, dark, confident, with breathing room.

References: High-end automotive detailer studios, premium concierge apps.
Anti-references: Basic cleaning services landing pages, cheap quick-job vibes, generic template sites, overly playful or corporate looks.

### Design Principles
1. **Luxury through restraint** — generous whitespace, limited color palette, no visual noise.
2. **Trust through clarity** — every label, price, and step is explicit. No hidden fees, no surprises.
3. **Mobile-first conversion** — the booking flow must feel effortless on a phone. Every field must earn its place.
4. **Premium over flashy** — subtle transitions, refined typography, and intentional spacing beat animations and effects.
5. **Consistency is credibility** — same spacing scale, same type hierarchy, same interaction patterns across every page.
